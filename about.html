About

A lab where science and the arts meet.

We bring researchers and artists into one space to study, design, and deliver programming that measurably improves brain, body, and mental health.

Who We Are

Evidence-based art, made in the community.

The Arts Wellness Lab is a community-based facility in Greenwich Village where scientists and artists collaborate to research and create evidence-based programming—powered by music, theater, dance, visual arts, and writing.

Our work spans the full arc from research to delivery: optimizing existing science into effective interventions, fast-tracking those programs into community and virtual settings, and measuring outcomes so we can show—not just claim—that doses of art improve health. Along the way we build a community of teaching artists, lower health care costs, ease the burden on caregivers, and give every demographic the chance to heal and grow through the arts.
